Privacy Policy – WageWise

Effective Date: August 28, 2025 · Last Updated: August 28, 2025

No Data Collected for Tracking or Advertising

WageWise ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the WageWise mobile application ("App").

Summary: WageWise is designed to work on your device. The data you enter (such as wages, hours, and expenses) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us, unless you explicitly choose to export or back up your data using services you control.

1) Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data from you by default. Specifically:

  • Account & Identity: No account is required; we do not collect your name, email, phone number, or identifiers.
  • Wage & Work Data: Entries you add (e.g., daily wages, hours worked, expenses) remain on your device.
  • Tracking & Ads: The App does not use third‑party advertising or tracking SDKs.

If you enable optional features (e.g., manual export, backups to your own cloud provider), your data will be handled by those third‑party services under their respective privacy policies.

2) How Your Information Is Used

  • To provide core App functionality such as recording wages, hours, and expenses on your device.
  • To support optional features you initiate (e.g., export to file, backup to your cloud account).

We do not sell, rent, or share your data for marketing or advertising.

3) Data Storage & Security

  • On‑device storage: Your entries are saved locally on your device by default.
  • Backups & Sync (optional): If you choose to back up or sync via services like iCloud/Google Drive, those providers store your data under their terms and security.
  • Safeguards: We take reasonable measures to keep the App secure; however, no method of storage is 100% secure.

4) Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • With your consent: When you export or share data.
  • Legal requirements: If required to comply with law or valid legal process.
  • Service providers (if used in the future): Only to the extent necessary to operate the App, in accordance with this Policy.

5) Your Choices & Controls

  • Access & Edit: View and edit your entries within the App.
  • Delete: You may delete entries from within the App or uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data from your device.
  • Backups: Enable or disable any optional backup/export features at any time.

6) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7) International Users

If you use optional cloud backups or exports, your data may be processed and stored by providers in other countries subject to their policies and applicable laws.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the App or legal requirements. We will update the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised Policy.
